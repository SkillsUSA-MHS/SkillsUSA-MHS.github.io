About Us

SkillsUSA is a partnership of students, teachers and industry working together to ensure America has a skilled workforce. SkillsUSA helps each student excel.

SkillsUSA Mililani - Our Purpose

SkillsUSA improves the quality of Americaâ€™s skilled workforce through a structured program of citizenship, leadership, employability, technical and professional skills training. SkillsUSA enhances the lives and careers of students, instructors and industry representatives as they strive to be champions at work.

At Mililani High School, we strive to fulfill this purpose by giving all our members a platform to train their attitudes, values, and skills as champions of the 21st century.

Our Sections

In SkillsUSA, we offer numerous fields of studies where you can train and compete in.

Graphics

Graphics tests your creativity through competition and evaluates contestant's design and aplication for excellence and professionalism in the field of graphic imaging.

Mobile Robotics

With an emphasis on competition, robotics will challenge your ability to preform, exhibit, and compile skills and knowledge through a unique contest objective.

Drafting | Technical Math

Drafting and Technical Math evaluates your knowledge of elaborate mathamatical theory and problem solving abilities through a written exam and complex architectural problem in competition.

Digital Media

Digital Media tests your knowledge and application of media-related skills in a unique and active competition.

Networking

Networking will push you to solve complex theory problems, test your physical mastery of cable-making, and challenge your troubleshooting abilities in a real-life situation.

Statesman

Statesman teaches you the purpose and structure behind SkillsUSA, and tests your leadership skills and mastery of certain requirements in leadership training.
