Kiawe Wood Pickup

The Kiawe Wood Pickup is a volunteer event on November 11th, where students will be collecting chopped wood for our later Imu Fundraiser.

November 11th, Forms go to G104

The Kiawe wood pickup is an annual event we complete in preparation for our Quarter 3 Imu Fundraiser.

The event was a major success even with the small number of attendees. We were able to pickup and unload two truckloads of kiawe wood to our agriculutural building in record time under the direction of advisors and students.

Beyond our organization, we also recieved assistance from our schools superb Janitorial staff to not only chop wood, but also get us in contact with the sponsor who graciously permitted us to chop two Kiawe wood trees on his property.
